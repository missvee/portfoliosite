About

Virginia has experience working in branding identity, digital advertising and technology startups across America and Denmark. She has a varied skillset in print, web and UI/UX.

Her work has been featured on AIGA Member Gallery and MICA Portfolios. She has received awards from Scholastic Art and Writing Awards, the National Foundation for Advancement in the Arts and School of the Art League.

She has worked with a variety of clients both big and small. A few of her clients include:

Brandtuitive, Fiaba Creative, Humanscale Design, IDEO, Jennergy, Macy's, MTV, PlaceIQ, Skift, Sideways NYC, Time Inc, Thinkso, Vaynermedia

When Virginia is not designing she is traveling around the globe or perfecting her handlettering.

sikker

A branding proposal for an asylum center in Copenhagen, Denmark. SIKKER is a center designed to aid refugees seeking asylum in Copenhagen. It provides Danish classes and a bike share to help residents assimilate to their new environment.

These are the architectural plans and elevations of Le Brut. The interior will be mostly gray and beige with modern furniture. The overall capacity of the restaurant will hold 55 people. The bar will serve juices and smoothies during the day and alcohol at night.

v1 gallery

V1 Gallery is an art space located in the meatpacking district of Copenhagen, Denmark. The gallery showcases emerging and established artists. Not only is the work presented to locals but to an international audience as well.

"V1 gallery is proud to present contemporary and cutting edge art to the Danish public."

I used the logo in the promotional posters for upcoming shows. Instead of showing the entire artwork, I decided to use the logo as a " peekhole " into the gallery's artwork in order to stir more interest from the viewing audience. Since the art seemed very contemporary and the audience is mostly those from the younger generation, I decided to go for a young and hip appearance.

Quantify Care

Quantified Care is a mobile health start up. They are committed to helping clinicians and their patients learn about and adopt evidence-based mobile medical apps and devices that have the potential to improve quality of care while lowering costs. Our mission is to bring these cutting-edge tools to a variety of clinical settings, from home health care to concierge medicine to global health.

I began my branding process with the following list of words that Quantified Care associated with.

Technical

Connections (Patients to Providers)

New/Unconventional

Reliable

Professional

Validated

Confidence

From these words, I abstracted the meaning and began to take it to a more visual level.

The four colored bars you see represent 4 arms colliding with eachother, they represent the idea of team work, unity and togetherness. A corner has been folded out to create the Q in Quantified Care.

Organize your life

On the front of each card there are icons that relate to the quotes on the back. The user can play a game of matching or organizing with the cards based on the icons. The cards can be sorted based on shapes or categories.

SPICES MIX FOR CELESTIAL SEASONING

The issue to address in this project was the preservation of spices along with promoting consumers to try new ones.

Here is a series of spices mix for Celestial Seasoning. Each spices mix features 6 - 8 different spices. The consumer can use each spice on its own or mix it according to the recipe provided. There are 3 different flavors, Cajun, Curry and Italian. The spices mix comes with cooking suggestions. Each mix provides one - three servings of seasoning, this allows for immediate use of the spices and prevents the spices from spoiling. Once the consumer has finished the spices, they can keep the recipe card as reference of the ingredients and serving suggestions for future uses.

cl9ud

CL9UD is a twin pack of tissues, it comes with 10 tissues per pack. The tissues are packed in a foldable compartment that is held together by a slit tab function. Once the user is finished with one pack, they can tear out the side of the empty carton and still have a pack that is convenient to carry. The packaging itself requires no glue at all. It's all held together by folds and tabs. It can be packed and easily transported in flat sheets and in huge quantities.

denmark promotional guide

Having lived in Denmark, I've grown quite fond of the country. I designed a guide that encourages people to visit Denmark. The pamphlet is a folded booklet that opens up into poster. Each " module " showcases reasons why people should check out Denmark and suggestions of things to do and places to see. It will be placed at a travel agency for people to browse through and see what possible destinations one should venture to. The user can take the booklet with them since it will be small and travel size friendly. In a sense, this print is designed as a promotional flyer but transformed into a visual poster for one's home.
