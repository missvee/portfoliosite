SPICES MIX FOR CELESTIAL SEASONING

The issue to address in this project was the preservation of spices along with promoting consumers to try new ones. I designed a series of spices mix for Celestial Seasoning. Each spices mix features 6 - 8 different spices. The consumer can use each spice on its own or mix it according to the recipe provided. There are 3 different flavors, Cajun, Curry and Italian. The spices mix comes with cooking suggestions. Each mix provides one - three servings of seasoning, this allows for immediate use of the spices and prevents the spices from spoiling. Once the consumer has finished the spices, they can keep the recipe card as reference of the ingredients and serving suggestions for future uses.
