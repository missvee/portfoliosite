MY STORY

Virginia Yu is a design creative specializing in branding & advertising. She provides services in graphic design such as print design, visual design, social media design, web design, production design and package design. She was born and bred in New York City. She received her BFA as a Presidential Scholar from the Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA). She spent a year abroad in Copenhagen, Denmark during her studies at the Danish Institute for Study Abroad (DIS). She is now currently based in Seattle. When she's not designing she is out seeking for inspiration from her travels or learning another language. She currently speaks English, Spanish, Cantonese and Danish.

A few of her clients include:

Brandtuitive, Dreamit Ventures, Humanscale Design, IDEO, Macy's, MTV, PLACEIQ, Quantified Care, Seidenfaden Design Copenhagen, Sideways Inc, Skift, Thinkso, Time Inc and Vaynermedia.
