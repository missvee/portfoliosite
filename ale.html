ALEWIFE BALTIMORE

Alewife Baltimore is a restaurant known for their burgers and beers. I rebranded and redesigned their logo based on their current color palate. The website has been modified to be more simple, with easier navigation. Alewife Baltimore is open during late hours, they serve lunch, dinner and late night meals along with their beers. With that in mind, they have a large variety of dishes to choose from. The drop down menu navigation makes it easier for the user to find what they want prior to arriving to the restaurant. A slideshow of their best dishes are provided for potential customers.
